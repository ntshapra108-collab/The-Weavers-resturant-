A Warm Welcome Awaits in Loughrea

Located on the historic Main Street of Loughrea, The Weaver's Rest has become a beloved gathering place for locals and visitors alike. Our cafe celebrates the rich culinary heritage of County Galway with a modern touch.

Whether you're seeking a hearty Full Irish Breakfast to start your day, a fresh Caesar Salad for lunch, or simply a perfect cup of Bewley's coffee with friends, you'll find it here, served with genuine Irish hospitality.
